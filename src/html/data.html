Data Insights

ManpowerGroup Solutions set out to uncover what impacts the candidate experience during a job search, including candidate behaviors and motivators when looking for employment. The results revealed important trends impacting retention and recruitment and the fact that job candidates around the world are surprisingly similar, from what drives them to job search, the tools they use, the challenges they face and the extent to which companies play a role in their hiring decisions.

Background

Research began in 2014 with just North America. Now, the Global Candidate Preferences Survey has expanded exponentially and includes results from 19 influential employment markets around the world.

Explore Map

Respondents

The job seekers surveyed were between 18 and 65 years old and currently in the workforce (not retired or homemakers). They represent a cross-section of age, income, employment status (full-time, part-time or contract), career level and industry.

Insights

A series of insights reports has been developed to reveal and compare global findings with country-based differences and generational nuances. They include important learnings into many aspects of the job search experience.

  • Career decision drivers
  • Motivations & challenges
  • Job search methods
  • Information known prior to applying
  • Feelings about recruitment
  • Preferred interview formats
